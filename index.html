About me

Hello, my name is Pete Higgins and I am an experienced ruby developer from Seattle. Besides programming I enjoy a good beer, an awful pun, other people's dogs, and weird internet humor. My current technical interest is studying functional programming through practicing haskell.

Experience

I have been developing web applications with ruby and rails for close to a decade. Before that I loved python and bash, and before that I did my undergraduate degree in computer science from BSU with c, c++ and java. In my career I've been the head of QA and a team lead, and I've worked at a government agency, a large corporation, small startups, and a consultancy. I am a co-organizer of the Seattle Ruby Brigade (Seattle.rb).
